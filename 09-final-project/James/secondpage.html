About Me

This website is intended as the final project of ARCH 6340 - Social Justice and the City with Prof. Jay Cephas in Northeastern University.

Dehong Zhang (James) is currently enrolled in the Master of Architecture at Northeastern University in Boston. He had accomplished a Bachelor of Architecture in Northeastern as well from the past five years. Growing up in Shenzhen which is one of the most populated cities in China, Zhang was fascinated by the rapidly growing city along with all the newly constructed skyscrapers.
