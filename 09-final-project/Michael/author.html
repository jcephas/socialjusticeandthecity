Michael Lydon


The author of this page, Michael Lydon, is a current Northeastern University Graduate Student in his final semester of his 3 Year Architectural Master's Program (Expected year of Graduation 2021) . He previously graduated from Boston College in 2018 with a Bachelor's Degree in English & History. Having grown up just South of Boston, and now currently in Boston he has an understanding of the uniqueness and character of his city.

Michael's focus in this project puts unification and understanding at the forefront of achieving justice for all. With his design and architectural skills, and familiarity with the Boston area, he hopes to open day be a key figure in implementating changes that will benefit the people as a whole.

For any information or questions regarding this site, or in general, please contact: lydon.m@northeastern.edu
