Pruitt-Igoe: St. Louis, Missouri

Pruitt Igoe was a housing project in St. Louis, which intended to solve the disparity between suburban and city living. However, its failure is attested to the lack of maintenance and responsibility that the developers and architects willfully ignored. In this case, the harm was caused through this lack of afterthought that led to its declining state and ultimate demolition. By putting people in homes in which they did not have the funds to upkeep, without providing external support, its inhabitants were left to its despair.
