The Body & Identity

This section explores the ways in which our identities are deeply, and complexly intertwined with our bodies. In extension, it further explores how race and gender are influenced and defined through these constraints. The relationship between society and our bodies highlight the many ways in which they can fail us, especially if one does not identify with its assosciated characteristics.
